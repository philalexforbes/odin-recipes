Tater Tot Casserole

Description

A simple dish that is easy to make and a family favorite.

You can even add cheese if you desire to give it some extra texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 16oz can of green beans
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 3 cups frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 350
  2. Brown the ground beef and drain
  3. Mix the ground beef, green beans, and cream of mushroom soup in a large bowl
  4. Pour the mixture into a 9x9 or 9x13 baking dish
  5. Spread out the tater tots evenly on the mixture in the pan
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until the tater tots are golden brown
  7. Remove and cool before eating
