Tacos

Description

A delicious and simple recipe that is great with hot sauce, cheese, and other ingredients.

You can also use different types of tortillas depending on your tastes. You can also use other ground meats as a substitute.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1 16 ounce jar of salsa
  • 1 ounce of taco seasoning
  • 1 package of tortillas of your choice

Steps

  1. In a large/medium skillet brown and drain your ground beef
  2. Add the refried beans, taco seasoning, and salsa mix well
  3. Cook on medium heat until the liquid has been mostly cooked down
  4. Grab the tortillas and your fixings
